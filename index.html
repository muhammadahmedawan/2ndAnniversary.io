How we met?

29 June 2020

I known each other since childhood. We were cousins, but since we lived in different countries, we had never had the chance to get to know each other better. That all changed in June 2020 when Faiza's family came to Pakistan from America for a visit. We all gathered together, and I finally had the chance to get to know her. We talked, laughed and shared stories about our lives. She was a sweet, kind and intelligent girl and I was drawn to her immediately. I spent more than 15 days with her family as a family member, but the last week together was different in a sense to collect more memories, enjoying each other's company. We had so much fun, and I realized that I had developed feelings for her and family as well. Unfortunately, the coronavirus pandemic struck, and there were very few flights available for Faiza and her family to travel back to America. So, they had to leave Pakistan earlier than planned. I was devastated that they had to go. Faiza and I kept in touch after they left, and over time, my feelings for her grew stronger. Eventually, our families agreed to let us be married, and we started planning our wedding. We were married in a beautiful ceremony surrounded by our families and friends. We looked back on those 7 days (a week) we spent together in Pakistan and smiled. We knew that our love story had begun there, and it was only fitting that we got married in the same country. We look forward to many more years of happiness together, and we will never forget those days in June 2020 that changed our lives forever.
